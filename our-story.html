Our Story

From a simple idea to New Zealand's trusted chauffeur service. Discover the Warm Voyage journey.

How It All Began

Warm Voyage was born from a simple observation: visitors to New Zealand deserved more than just transportation—they deserved a warm, welcoming experience from the moment they arrived.

The Beginning

Founded in 2020, Warm Voyage started with one vehicle and a commitment to providing exceptional service. Our founder, having experienced the challenges of navigating a new country, wanted to make arriving in New Zealand stress-free and enjoyable.

Growing Together

Through word-of-mouth and dedication to excellence, we expanded our fleet and team. Today, we serve thousands of clients across Auckland and Queenstown, maintaining the same personal touch that started it all.

Looking Forward

We continue to grow while staying true to our core values. Our vision is to become New Zealand's most trusted chauffeur service, known for reliability, warmth, and going above and beyond for every client.
